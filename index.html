About Us

SV Hollow Blocks is a domestic block manufacturer that produces hollow and concrete blocks. It was founded in 1998. It is located in Thirumenahalli,Bengaluru. The company was first well-known for its hollow blocks, which gradually gave way to concrete blocks in response to consumer demand. The business is committed to fulfilling the different demands of the market. In addition, SV Hollow Blocks provides the aggregated stone and sand needed for construction.

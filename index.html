About Us

SV Hollow Blocks established in 1998 is a homegrown block manufacturing company involving in both concrete and hollow blocks it is situated in Thirumenhalli Bengaluru. Initially the comapany was known for its hollow blocks which slowly evolved to concrete blocks as per the market needs. The company is determined to meet the various market requirements. SV Hollow Blocks is also the suppliers of the stone and sand aggregated which fulfils construction needs.
